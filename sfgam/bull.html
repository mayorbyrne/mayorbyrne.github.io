The Story:

A ferocious beast once terrorized old Southwest Territory, inciting the citizens there to flee the town and build the beautiful courtyards and mission seen today. The raging bull still lurks in the ruins of the old mission, which now serves as the loading station for this wild, unpredictable roller coaster ... Raging Bull.

The Specs for Raging Bull:

Introduced

Spring 1999

First Drop

200 feet

Length

5,057 feet

Top Speed

73 mph

Ride Duration

approx. 2 minutes, 30 seconds

Number of Trains

3 (36 per train)

Number of Cars

9

Colors

Structure is wine-colored, track is orange, trains are bright teal, red and yellow with a bovine print motif and bull's horns

Special Features

Riders are secured in unique, open-sided cars by lap-bar restraint. Features 6 steep-banked turns and inclined loops.

Height Restriction

Must be 54" to ride

Manufacturer

Bolliger & Mabillard
