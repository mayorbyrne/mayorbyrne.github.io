Batman...The Ride...

The line for this ride is the best in the park, because you go through Gotham City. The ride is excellent and fast, but over very quickly. The suspended chairs make the ride, but the loops and corkscrews also help. The lift itself is longer than the rest of the ride, but when I get off the ride, I don't hear anybody complaining. A great thrill with an unusual non-menacing wait.
