The Rides

  • River Rocker - A cheesy ride located in Southwest territory. You sit in a canoe and fly back and forth, like the pirate ships you often see at carnivals. If you like to get a little queezy, go on it.

  • Hay Baler - This ride is sometimes hard to locate. Nestled in the "woods", it spins you around and around and around. A boring ride that is good to go on if you are waiting for someone on a ride.

  • Sky Whirl - A classic ride at Six Flags. There has been some talk about removing it from the park. A three wayy "ferris - wheel" that lasts for about fifteen minutes. A great time - buyer.
