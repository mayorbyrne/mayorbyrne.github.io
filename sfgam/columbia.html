The Rides

  • Columbia Carousel - A giant, double-decker carousel that's a lot of fun to go on, especially at night.
  • Dino Island II: Escape From Dino Island 3D - Combining realistic 3D animation with dramatic surround sound, this blockbuster turbo ride offers an exciting, in-your-face experience! (Info taken from Official site.)
  • Sky Trek Tower - A "towering" tower that sits over 200 feet above Great America. You go up on a circular sitting platform that rotates and is surrounded by glass.
  • Scenic Railway - A peaceful train ride throughout Great America that notes popular stops and has 2 points where you can get off. But, be prepared for screams as this peaceful railway turns into a delightful tour of "Coroner Bros." Studio during Fright Fest.
  • Triple Play - A thrilling ride that rotates you pretty high and then hovers back down to the earth.
