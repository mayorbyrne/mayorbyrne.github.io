The American Eagle, the classic white coaster in front of Great America. This rickety coaster will throw you from your seat and shake you all the way to the end. Its massive 150 foot drop is one of the largest in the park, and this one goes STRAIGHT down. The spirals in the middle of the ride let the coaster pick up speed, and then you will go through a few more mini-drops before ending. This is one classic that will take your breath away.
