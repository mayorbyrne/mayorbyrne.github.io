The Rides

  • Fiddler's Fling - This is like "teacups" on steroids. Don't forget to bring a barf-bag!
  • Daredevil Dive - Pay your way on (pretty expensive). Bungee Jump from about 120 feet. Pretty exciting, but I wouldn't do it again.
  • Logger's Run - The best water ride at the park. A basic log flume ride gone mad. There are mini-drops during the ride and a towering one at the end. prepare to get soaking wet!
  • Whirligig - This one made it to my ride of the month section before I stopped working on my page for about 3 or 4 months. An excellent ride to ride on at night, it is just a basic swing ride. I have fun on it, and I think you would too.
  • Yankee Clipper - Another good water ride similar to Logger's Run, but you don't get as wet on this one. But, you better know what days this ride is open, because it is closed almost every time I go to the park.
