  • By the way, I have received numerous emails about height restriction on the "big rides." Batman and Iron Wolf both have a height restriction of 54 inches. The Giant Drop has a height restriction of 48 inches. The American Eagle, The Demon, and The Whizzer all have a height restriction of 42 inches. You must be 54 inches to ride Raging Bull.
