The Demon...A rollercoaster that provides thrills, without the usual wait of Shockwave. Basically a mini-Shockwave, it probably has the best and worst drop in the entire park, for now, depending on how you think. The rider is thrust into two loops, then sent screaming into a lighted tunnel. After slowing down, the rider is sent into a double corkscrew where he or she is finally stopped. A short but great ride, I urge you to challenge The Demon.
