The Rides

  • East River Crawler - This is more of a kiddie ride that throws you up and down. Not much fun if you are used to coasters.

  • Cajun Cliffhanger - A ride that spins you around fast and drops the floor. You stick to the wall, and if you're inexperienced, you will get very sick.

  • Roaring Rapids - No matter what time of year, this one always get you wet. Simulating white water rafting, it gets you soaked if they run the waterfall and water jets. Another note: this ride turns into Blood River during Fright Fest.

  • Condor - This ride is like the scrambler, only it moves up a long pole, and then brings you down. One word: boring.
  • Power Dive - Like River Rocker, only this ride goes upside down. Oh, man, does the blood rush to your head on this one. Not for the faint of heart.
